Coding Seekho

A paragraph is a unit of writing that expresses a single main idea, typically consisting of a topic sentence, supporting sentences, and a concluding sentence. Definition and Purpose: A paragraph is a group of sentences that form a coherent unit, focusing on one main idea or theme, often called the controlling idea, which guides the content of the paragraph (Lunsford and Connors).
